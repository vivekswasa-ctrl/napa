Overview

What is NAPA?

NAPA began as a place for pulmonologists to speak openly, beyond slide-heavy CME with room for dialogue on practice, wellbeing, and life as a clinician.

Talks range from clinical questions to the practical side of work: administration, finances, and balance with peers who share the specialty.

150+ members now take part; senior colleagues mentor newer ones on practice and planning, and the network has helped with roles and placements too.

Our meetings are grounded in dialogue about clinical practice, career choices, and life beyond the hospital, complemented by structured learning whenever the calendar allows.

Who we are

Built for doctors who breathe medicine and life

A space for genuine relationships, not only exchanging cards, but sharing difficult cases and honest perspective.

What we focus on

Gatherings, connections, and public voice

Our calendar is organised around three ideas: bring people together, help them grow, and elevate respiratory health in the public conversation — with members seen as credible voices.

Signature experiences

Annual forums, city meet-ups, curated dinners, and destination programmes designed as memorable chapters — not calendar clutter.

Structured networking

Introductions across metros, subspecialty interests, and career stages — informal when it helps, intentional when it counts.

Awareness & outreach

Respiratory health campaigns, education for the public, and media collaborations that position members as approachable experts.

NAPA’s promise: every programme is meant to add tangible value to your reputation as a clinician and quality of life as a person. Not tick box activity!

Member journey

Room to lead, learn, and be seen

Membership is designed for depth: voice in the field, research momentum, leadership inside the association, and fair access to industry dialogue — without turning medicine into marketing.

Voice & visibility

Platforms to speak, publish, and be recognised as a thoughtful contributor to respiratory medicine.

Research momentum

Co-authoring, multi-centre conversations, and national data efforts where your insight matters.

Lead from within

Shape chapters, co-create gatherings, and influence how the association evolves — not as a spectator.

Industry dialogue

Preferred routes to ethical pharma collaboration, advisory conversations, and innovation panels — with guardrails that protect credibility.

Doctor life

Wellbeing isn’t optional . It’s structural!

A healthy physician ecosystem is part of patient outcomes. NAPA invests in mental health literacy, movement, travel, and everyday perks that honour the weight of your work.

Mental wellness

Curated resources, peer circles, and expert-led sessions on stress, boundaries, and burnout prevention.

Active living

Challenges, sports meet-ups, and retreats that keep energy and camaraderie high.

Travel & leisure

Group travel experiences — domestic and international — planned for busy clinicians and their families where relevant.

Lifestyle partners

Select benefits across dining, stays, fitness, and brands — a small thank-you for big responsibility.
